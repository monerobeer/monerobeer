Monero.Beer

Monero is a tool that enables you to privately receive value over the internet

Simple

Download a monero wallet (e.g. iOS, Android & Desktop), label a sub-address and share it. No servers, no downtime.

Private receiving

Payments are made to Stealth Addresses, your donation address never appears on chain so no-one can tell if you received any donations.

Private balance

Amounts are hidden on monero, so no-one knows how much you receive (only the sender!). There is no risk leaking your full balance amount when making a payment.

Private spending

Even the sender can't tell when you spend the monero they donate thanks to Ring Signatures. This gives plausible deniability (you can claim you have spent when you haven't or claim you haven't spent when you have!)
