Monero.Beer

Monero is a tool that enables you to privately receive value (for beer?) over the internet

Simple

Download a monero wallet (e.g. iOS, Android & Desktop), label a sub-address and share it.

Reliable

No servers, no downtime.

Private

  • Payments are made to Stealth Addresses, your donation address never appears on chain so no-one can tell if you received any donations.
  • Amounts are hidden, so no-one knows how much you receive. There is no risk leaking your full balance amount when making a payment.
  • Even the sender can't tell when you spend the monero they donate thanks to Ring Signatures giving plausible deniability.

What does a subaddress look like?

Sub-addresses start with an 8, for example:

83nNJxLgDZaL1L4sJSP6G2N3vYEyJ2RxFS5tMdWpsyvnKPraKLQJpgmjKeRcPYjJZ4F1kemTRXW7QQbYAaJAt1Nj6A4SmZR

Always use a different subaddress for each identity / payer.
